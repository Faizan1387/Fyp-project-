Gas Sensors


Three kinds of gas sensors will be used:
MQ-2 for sensing Carbon Monoxide CO.
MQ-7 for sensing Nitrogen Oxides NOx.
MQ-135 for sensing Flammable gases.
These sensors will detect specific gases from the smoke of the vehicle and send the concentration to Arduino.

ESP 32

ESP32 is the name of the chip that was developed by Espressif Systems. This provides Wi-Fi (and in some models) dual-mode Bluetooth connectivity to embedded devices. While ESP32 is technically just the chip, modules and development boards that contain this chip are often also referred to as “ESP32” by the manufacturer. It is responsible for collecting data from the gas sensors and then sending it to Firebase.

BreadBoard

This breadboard is used to make common connection. To connect all sensors,esp 32. A breadboard, solderless breadboard, or protoboard is a construction base used to build semi-permanent prototypes of electronic circuits. Unlike a perfboard or stripboard, breadboards do not require soldering or destruction of tracks and are hence reusable.

Jumper Wires

umper wires are used for making connections between items on your breadboard and your esp 32's header pins. Use them to wire up all your circuits. Use to connect esp32 and gas sensors.
