Real-time monitoring:

The purposed system will provide real-time monitoring of vehicle emissions, allowing authorities to quickly identify areas with high levels of pollution and take action to reduce emissions

Alert system:

The purposed system will be able to generate alerts when vehicles exceed predefined pollution limits, allowing authorities to take action against non-compliant vehicles.

Public awareness:

The system should raise public awareness about the importance of reducing vehicle emissions and the impact of air pollution on health and the environment. Improved air quality: The ultimate goal of the system is to improve air quality by reducing vehicle emissions and promoting the use of clean transportation technologies. In summary, an IoT-based vehicle pollution monitoring and alert system can help reduce air pollution, ensure compliance with regulations, save costs, improve data collection, and future-proof the system for the adoption of cleaner vehicles. These justifications highlight the importance and necessity of developing and implementing such a system.
